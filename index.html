About Us

Brekio is a 4th generation platform that leverages the components of the blockchain technology to provide transparency and reliability to its users.

Brekio is a decentralized freelancing platform that facilitates the use of intelligent programmable contracts to execute projects between freelancers and their customers. Brekio dismantles the existing third-party broker model used within the freelancing industry. Through blockchain technology, Brekio introduces transparency and efficiency to the existing freelancing landscape.

Brekio Features

Immediate Receipt of Payments via Cryptocurrencies Ensuring Trust and Transparency for Freelancer and Customer

Private BLOCKCHAIN Network

Brekio is built on a private blockchain network with military grade security protocols, offering complete control, regulatory compliance, and immediate transaction confirmations.

Smart Contracts

Brekio employs intelligent programmable contracts for quicker execution of projects, transparency, clear communication, efficiency, and to avoid manipulation from either party.

Secure Online Payments

Brekio secures all the transactions cryptographically, eliminating any chances of a hack or suspicious interpretation of data. It utilizes trusted third-party payment networks for fiat settlements.

Payment Protection

Brekio offers payment protection to freelancers by depositing funds in an escrow account. We guarantee that the freelancer gets paid for every tracked hour.

Instant Payments with Cryptocurrencies

Brekio facilitates immediate payment settlements through cryptocurrencies. We support all the major cryptocurrencies with free settlements available for Brekio tokens.

Changing Traditional Intermediary Model

Brekio introduces a flat 8% fee for every project completed on its platform. It breaks the traditional 20% fee-model, which is followed by leading freelancing platforms.

The Freelance
Revolution is Transforming the Global Work Culture

The Freelance economy is rewriting the rules of traditional employment around the world. The global freelance industry is worth over $1.5 trillion.

The US alone has over 57 million freelancers, representing nearly 36% of its workforce. More than 50% of the American workforce will become a part of the freelance economy by 2027.

A growing number of Fortune 500 companies are employing freelancers at different levels.

Freelancers around the world are reportedly happier than their employed counterparts. Millennials are the flag-bearers of the global freelance economy.
